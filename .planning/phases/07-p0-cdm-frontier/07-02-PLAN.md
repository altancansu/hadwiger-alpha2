DFS reference contract (07-RESEARCH.md §"The DFS reference decision procedure" — copy that body):
- `has_cdm(adj, n) -> list[tuple[int,int]] | None` — witness M (edges a<b) or None.
- Two private definition helpers (the CDM analog of corpus/verifier._is_conflict):
  `vsets_adjacent(e, f)`  → for disjoint edges e={a,b}, f={c,d}: `c in adj[a] or d in adj[a] or c in adj[b] or d in adj[b]`
  `dominates(M)`          → every w ∉ V(M) is adjacent to ≥1 endpoint of EACH M-edge (A1 reading, LOCKED)
- DFS: seed over all edges; each added edge must (i) cover a currently-undominated vertex,
  (ii) stay a matching, (iii) be pairwise V-adjacent to all current M-edges (keeps M "connected").

CP-SAT encoding (07-RESEARCH.md §"The CP-SAT cross-check encoding" — copy that model):
- `x[e]` Bool per G-edge; per-vertex AddAtMostOne (matching); `sum(x) >= 1` (non-empty);
  pairwise `x[e]+x[f] <= 1` for disjoint non-V-adjacent edge pairs (connected);
  `x[e] <= sum(inc_w)` for each w non-adjacent to both endpoints of e (dominating).
