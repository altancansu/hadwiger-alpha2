Two ingredients (from RESEARCH):
1. CLWY Lemma 2.5 [CITED arXiv:2512.17114]: for triangle-free G on ≥3 vertices, TFAE —
   (i) edge-maximal triangle-free; (ii) diameter 2; (iii) complement α=2 and edge-minimal;
   (iv) complement has no dominating edge. Empirically confirmed: `tfp.is_edge_maximal_tf`
   over the raw geng stream reproduced 61 (n=11) / 147 (n=12), identical to `pickg -Z2`.
2. CDM edge-addition monotonicity (prove in-repo): CDM(G) ⟹ CDM(G+e) — M's edges are unchanged;
   "connected" and "dominating" are adjacency-positive conditions and adjacency only increases.

Open Q1 obligation (do NOT gloss): monotonicity flows minimal ⟹ supergraphs; some edge-minimal
α=2 graphs are DISCONNECTED (complement = complete bipartite ⟹ G = K_a⊔K_b) and legitimately
fail CDM without being counterexamples (Conjecture 10 hypothesizes CONNECTED G). Recommended
phrasing: prove directly over connected G via a connectivity-preserving maximal-triangle-free
extension of H=Ḡ; catalog the disconnected complements as out-of-scope for the connected frontier.

Executable predicate analog: src/alpha2/generators/tfp.py:56-62 (`is_edge_maximal_tf(adj, n)`),
`has_cdm` from 07-02. test_transfer_lemma was scaffolded RED in 07-01.
