<objective>
Build the g(G) certificate persistence leg: a schema that records `g = (χ − had₃)/χ` and states
ONLY what a proven `had₃ < χ` establishes ("no K_χ minor with branch sets ≤3" — NOT `had(G) < χ`),
a stdlib-only verifier that re-derives and re-checks the claim from stored data (fails closed under
`python -O`), and an append-only verify-at-append store isolated from the frozen corpora. This is
the certificate-honesty backbone the whole break-hunt rests on.

Purpose: make the radioactive-impossibility discipline structural — a g(G) record physically cannot
be persisted unless its claim re-verifies and its statement is honest.
Output: `pool/sumfree/{schema,verifier,store}.py`. Turns test_schema / test_store / the
certificate-honesty half of test_screen GREEN.
</objective>
